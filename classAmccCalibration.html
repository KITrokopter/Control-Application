Detailed Description

AmccCalibration.h.

multi camera calibration with amcc toolbox used method of M. Warren, D. McKinnon, B. Upcroft, "Online Calibration of Stereo Rigs for Long-Term Autonomy", in International Conference on Robotics and Automation, Karlsruhe, Germany, 2013.
